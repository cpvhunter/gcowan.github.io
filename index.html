Bio: I am an STFC Ernest Rutherford Fellow at the University of Edinburgh (grant ST/K004646/1). My research focuses on looking for signs of CP violation and exotic hadron spectroscopy at the LHCb experiment at CERN. I also study novel photo-sensors for the future long-baseline neutrino experiment Hyper-Kamiokande, in Japan. I teach on the Edinburgh Numerical Recipes course and occassionally lecture particle physics to the Masters students.
